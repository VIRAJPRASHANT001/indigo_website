At INDIGO CATERING & SERVICES, we are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for our clients and their teams.Since our inception in 2008, we have expanded our footprints across india.Providing exceptional catering and facility management services tailored to diverse business needs.

At Indigo, we believe that true success lies not in the volume of our work, but in the satisfaction of our customers and clients. Our unwavering commitment to quality is what drives us forward.



Company overview

Founded in 2008, Indigo Catering & Services Pvt. Ltd. offers top -notch catering and facilities management across india. We Excel in Corporate,Remote,and institutional catering.as well as facilities management. Our expert team is dedicated to delivering exceptional food and services, catering experiances that foster community and well being.
